Logline

A multi-hyphenate who treats every medium as a place to tell the truth — beautifully.

Cas Sigers began in costume design, dressing touring stage productions, before a single treatment turned into a screenwriting career that now spans more than twenty-five produced films and series.

A North Carolina native based in Atlanta, Cas writes the feel-good family stories and holiday hits networks rely on — then turns around and directs gritty crime, suspense, and thrillers. Her work has aired on BET, TV One, Hallmark, UPtv, AMC+, ALLBLK, Lifetime, and streaming platforms worldwide.

Alongside actress Terri J. Vaughn she co-founded the production company Nina Holiday Entertainment — named for Nina Simone and Billie Holiday — with a clear mission: create quality stories centered on women who look like them, and mentor the next generation of writers and directors into the room.

The books

A novelist across Kensington and Urban Books — and the author behind comedian Katt Williams's national best-selling memoir.

A novelist first.

Cas's debut, A Girl Named Lily, set the career in motion — it landed in the hands of Terri J. Vaughn and, optioned for the screen, became the spark for their production company. Her fiction blends warmth, humor, and heart with characters readers recognize.

Her 2024 novel Sycamore Song arrived through Pen Legacy Publishing, the newest chapter in a body of work that moves as easily across bookstore shelves as her stories do across networks.

She also authored comedian Katt Williams's memoir, Nine Lives, No Wives and 69 Concubines — carrying her voice from fiction onto the celebrity-memoir shelf.
